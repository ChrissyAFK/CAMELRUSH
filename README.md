## CAMEL RUSH!!!!

WELCOME TO CAMEL RUSH
Camel Rush is a game where you try to finish the platformer as fast
as you can, before your Camel overheats and EXPLODES!! ^-^

## CONTROLS

W - Jump
A, D - Move Left/Right
E - Drink Water
SPACE - Spit to Attack
SHIFT - Fart Away!
UP/DOWN - Control Spit Direction
